About HKU PL Group

We are a group of programming language researchers who study topics about functional language design, type theory, compilers and program analysis.

Research

Our research interests cover a wide range of topics related to programming languages. We are creating a new experimental programming language, FCore. Check out our Github page for more ongoing projects.
